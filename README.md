Scanly

Scanly is a fast, privacy-focused document scanner for Android. It turns your phone into a powerful text extractor and barcode scannerâ€”completely offline.

Features

Extract Text from Images: accurately recognize text in photos using advanced OCR.

PDF Support: Convert entire PDF documents into editable text.

QR & Barcode Scanner: Instantly scan QR codes, barcodes, and more.

Multi-Language: Built-in support for English and Arabic.

Privacy First: 100% offline. Your data never leaves your device.

Modern Design: Material 3 interface with Dark Mode & OLED support.

How to Use

Open the App: You'll see the home screen with three main options.

Capture Photo: Take a picture of a document to extract text immediately.

From Gallery: Pick an existing image from your phone to scan.

Extract PDF: Select a PDF file to process all its pages at once.

Scan Barcode: Switch to the Barcode mode to scan QR codes or product barcodes.
